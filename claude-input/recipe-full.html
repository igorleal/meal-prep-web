Spicy Basil Chicken Stir-Fry

25 min

Ingredients

500g Chicken Breast Sliced into thin strips
2 tbsp Soy Sauce Low sodium preferred
1 cup Fresh Basil Ideally Holy Basil
3 cloves Garlic Minced
2 Bird's Eye Chilies Adjust for heat
1 tbsp Oyster Sauce Or mushroom sauce

Preparation

1

Prep the Chicken & Sauce

Slice the chicken breast into thin bite-sized pieces. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a pinch of sugar. Set aside.

2

Aromatize the Wok

Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced chilies. Stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

3

Cook the Chicken

Increase heat to high. Add the chicken slices and stir-fry vigorously for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and slightly browned on the edges.

4

Finish with Basil

Pour in the sauce mixture and toss to coat. Turn off the heat immediately and fold in the fresh basil leaves. The residual heat will wilt the basil perfectly. Serve hot over jasmine rice.
