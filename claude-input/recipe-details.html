Spicy Basil Chicken with Rice

Fridge Storage

Ingredients

  • check_circle 1.5 lbs Chicken breast, diced
  • radio_button_unchecked 2 cups Jasmine rice
  • radio_button_unchecked 1 cup Fresh Thai basil leaves
  • radio_button_unchecked 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • radio_button_unchecked 2 Red chili peppers, sliced
  • radio_button_unchecked 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • radio_button_unchecked 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • radio_button_unchecked 1 tsp Fish sauce (optional)

Preparation

1

Prepare the rice and ingredients

Rinse the jasmine rice until the water runs clear, then cook according to package instructions. While the rice is cooking, dice the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces. Mince the garlic and slice the red chili peppers.

2

Stir-fry the aromatics

Heat a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat with a tablespoon of oil. Add the minced garlic and sliced chilies. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

3

Cook the chicken

Add the chicken pieces to the wok. Stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and slightly browned on the edges. Ensure high heat to get a good sear.

4

Season and serve

Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and optional fish sauce. Toss well to coat. Turn off the heat and immediately stir in the basil leaves until wilted. Serve hot over the steamed jasmine rice.
